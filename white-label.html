How to remove the "Made on Tilda" credit from the footer.
The "Made on Tilda" credit is placed on all website pages by default. However, you may opt to remove it in the Site Settings if you have an active annual Tilda Personal Plan or Tilda Business Plan subscription.
If you have such a subscription, go to the Site Settings → More → Platform Label → Off. Save changes and republish all pages.
